The Best Movies According to Anushka

My top three movies of all time

Genre : Comedy Drama

3 Idiots

Two pals are looking for a long-lost friend. They reflect back to their time in college and remember their friend, who encouraged them to think creatively despite being labelled as "idiots" by the rest of the world.

Genre : Crime Drama Mystery

Drishyam

After his family commits an unforeseen crime, a father takes desperate steps to protect them from the law's dark side.

Genre : Fantasy

Avatar

A paraplegic Marine sent on a special mission to the moon Pandora finds himself divided between carrying out his instructions and defending the place he considers to be his home.
